📁 Dataset: Weekly Telecom Complaints (Time Series)

This dataset contains weekly complaint volumes for a telecom provider, along with marketing and promotional indicators.

Columns:

- week: The time period (weekly date or index)
- complaints: Number of customer complaints registered that week
- discount_rate: Discount percentage offered during the week
- small_commercial_event: Binary flag indicating a minor promo or campaign
- medium_commercial_event: Binary flag for a mid-level commercial event
- big_commercial_event: Binary flag for a major campaign (e.g., product launch or ad blitz)
